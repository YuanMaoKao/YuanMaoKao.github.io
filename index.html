About

I am an assistant professor of Operations Management at the Zicklin School of Business, Baruch College, CUNY. My current research focuses on dynamic control, demand learning, and mechanism design by pricing. Prior to joining Zicklin, I obtained my Ph.D. degree at Fuqua School of Business, Duke University. I also got a M.S. degree in Industrial Engineering and a B.B.A. degree in Business Administration from National Taiwan University.

Research Interests

  • Dynamic pricing, revenue management, supply chain management, operations-finance interface
  • Statistical learning, exploration-exploitation, mechanism design, consumer choice models
